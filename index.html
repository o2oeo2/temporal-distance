TEMPORAL DISTANCE

In modern society, daily life follows fixed time standards that feel natural and neutral. Yet individual rhythms often diverge from these expectations. This project visualises the relationship between socially structured time and personal time, allowing viewers to compare their own schedules with collective patterns. It does not seek to correct behaviour, but to create a space for reflection — encouraging individuals to look at their own daily rhythm and reconsider what "normal" time means.

HOW TO USE

This project visualises the gap between socially accepted time standards and individual daily rhythms. There is no right or wrong — only difference.
